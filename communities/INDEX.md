## How to Read Each Entry

Every platform is described along four axes:

- **Sign up** — what account / tier you need to start
- **Code & AI** — what you can build and which AI is embedded
- **Community** — how people interact (forums, PRs, competitions, Discord)
- **Ship** — how you go from prototype to live product
